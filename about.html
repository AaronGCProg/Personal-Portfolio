About Me

I always have been a videogame fan, passionate about it and always wanted to make of this industry my career. I consider myself specialized on videogame Production and Design; as my passion is in organizing, dealing with people and finding the best fit for a project by managing time, and on the other hand I love being able to conceive the architecture of the game and model the experience of the player.

 

I spend my free time on several different aspects, perhaps the least common of which would be: Longboarding, Airsoft and Snowboarding. I also love board games, tabletop role-playing, watching series and discovering new hobbies to at least try them once. Obviously what I spend more time on is playing videogames, researching new projects on the market and keeping up to date with the industry.

The passion I have for videogames comes from my childhood. I have always played games such as Medievil, Final Fantasy IX & Metal Gear Solid. The same question would forever round my head: "How do they make these videogames?"

Growing up I opened my mind as a versatile player, starting to like competitive games from Rainbow Six Siege to immersive and vast worlds such as The Witcher 3 or Divinity Original Sin 2, even strategy games like Total War Saga. Now, my goal is contribute to the development of projects as big as the ones mentioned and for others to play and enjoy them as I did.

As a producer and designer, I consider my strength to be my ability to work as a team; as well as suggest creative endeavors and give a solution to any obstacle we may find along the process.

About Me

I always have been a videogame fan, passionate about it and always wanted to make of this industry my career. I would like to specialize myself on videogame design or programming; as I love being able to conceive the architecture of the game and model the experience of the players.

The passion I have for videogames comes from my childhood. I have always played games such as Medievil, Final Fantasy IX, Metal Gear Solid, etc... The same question would forever round my head: "How do they make these videogames?"

Growing up I opened my mind as a player, starting to like competitive games from Rainbow Six Siege to immersive and vast worlds such as The Witcher 3. Now, my goal is to program and design the architecture of projects as big as the ones mentioned and for others to play and enjoy them as I did.

As a programmer and designer, I consider my strength to be my ability to work as a team; as well as suggest creative endeavors and give a solution to any obstacle we may find on the process.

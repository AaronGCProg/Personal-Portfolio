About Me

I have always been a fan of video games, deeply passionate about the industry and harbouring a long-standing ambition to forge a career within it. My expertise lies in video game production and design, where my passion for organisation, interpersonal dynamics as dealing with people, and project optimisation shines through. Moreover, I take great satisfaction in ensuring the perfect alignment between the architecture of games and model to enhance the player experience.

 

As a producer and designer, I excel in working with teams, coming up with creative ideas, and problem-solving in the gaming industry. Additionally, my creativity and adaptability shine through in my ability to conceptualize and analyze diverse gaming scenarios. My proactive nature is evident in my commitment to staying updated and my dedication to self-directed learning, constantly seeking out new knowledge to broaden my expertise.

My love for video games has been with me since childhood, driven by a fascination with a variety of gaming experiences. I have always been captivated by the creativity and innovation in game development, sparking a deep curiosity about how games are made and leading me towards a career in the field. As I have grown, my gaming interests have expanded to include a wide range of genres and gameplay styles. Whether it is competitive multiplayer, immersive storytelling, or strategic challenges, I have consistently sought out diverse gaming experiences. Now, I am eager to use my expertise to contribute to meaningful projects in the gaming industry, enriching the experiences of players worldwide and sharing the same sense of wonder and enjoyment that has fueled my own gaming journey.

In my leisure time, I engage in a diverse array of activities, from longboarding and airsoft to snowboarding. I also have a keen interest in board games, tabletop role-playing, watching series and exploring new hobbies, even if only to give them a try. However, my primary focus remains on gaming, where I continually immerse myself in playing, researching new trends, and staying abreast of industry developments.

Given my past experiences, interests, and growing admiration for the gaming sphere, I have found myself naturally drawn to the dynamic world of game development. This enthusiasm has shaped my career aspirations and provided invaluable insights as both a player and a developer. My expertise in game production and design is rooted in a genuine appreciation for the balance between structure and player experience. As I pursue my ambitions within the gaming industry, I am committed to leveraging my unique perspective as a passionate gamer to create immersive experiences for audiences worldwide.
